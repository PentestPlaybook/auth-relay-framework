1) WordpressRelayApp Android App:

a) Tap button 1 to verify Termux is Set Up.
(Note: If button 1 fails, ensure you completed the setup instructions in termux_setup.txt)

b) Connect your android to the pineapple AP.
(Note: Ensure your android shows an active Wi-Fi signal and your Pineapple has internet access)

c) Tap button 2 to verify your android is connected to your pineapple.

d) Tap button 3 to download wordpress-relay.py and establish SSH access to the Pineapple.
(Note: Your Pineapple SSH Password is required for the initial setup) 

e) Tap button 4 and enter your wordpress domain. Wait for the browser to open and connect to your WordPress site.
(Note: If Button 4 fails, make sure your WordPress Droplet allows inbound traffic on port 443.)
